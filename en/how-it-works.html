How it works

Discovering how much you're worth takes less than a minute. Here's how.

1

Bring all your assets together

Open the calculator and enter every source of money you have: checking, provident fund for investment, pension, study fund, money market fund, bank deposit, self-managed trading account, and even real estate or cash. Add as many rows as you like and give each one its own name.

2

Watch the picture take shape

The moment you enter an amount, your total net worth and the donut chart update instantly. Each asset gets a color and a proportional slice, so you can clearly see what most of your wealth rests on – and what takes up too little space.

3

Gain perspective

The visual picture turns scattered numbers into one clear understanding. Now you can ask the right questions: Am I too liquid? Is my retirement saving enough? Where should I diversify? This is the starting point for every good financial decision.

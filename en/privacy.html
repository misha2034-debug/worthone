Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Disclaimer: this document is a basic template for illustration and does not constitute legal advice.

1. The guiding principle: your data stays with you

Your privacy is at the heart of the product. The financial data you enter in the calculator (amounts and asset names) is processed solely within your browser. It is not sent to our servers, is not stored by us and is not exposed to any third party. To save you from re-entering it, the data is saved in your browser's local storage (localStorage) on your device – it stays with you only and never leaves the device. You can delete it at any time using the "Reset" button in the calculator or by clearing your browser data.

2. What data we do collect

We may collect anonymous, aggregated technical information to improve the service, for example:

  • General usage data (number of visitors, pages viewed) via analytics tools.
  • Basic technical information such as browser and device type.

This information does not identify you personally and does not include the financial data you entered.

3. Contact form

If you reach out via the contact page, we will use the details you provide (name and email) solely to respond to your inquiry.

4. Cookies

We may use essential cookies and analytics cookies. You can block cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as usual.

5. Your rights

Since we do not store your financial data, we hold no personal record about you. Regarding information provided in an inquiry, you have the right to review, correct or request its deletion – contact us to do so.

6. Changes to this policy

We will update this policy from time to time. The last updated date appears at the top of the page.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related question, you can reach us via the contact page.

About WorthOne

Your money is scattered across ten different places – it's time to see all of it in one picture.

Most of us spread our money across many places – a checking account at one bank, a provident fund at another company, a pension somewhere else, maybe some self-managed investments and a study fund. The result? Almost no one can confidently answer one simple question: how much am I worth?
Our vision

One clear picture, better decisions

WorthOne was born from the belief that everyone should be able to understand their financial situation without an economics degree and without complicated spreadsheets. We turn the scattered picture into one clear, visual view that lets you see yourself – and make better decisions.

What guides us

Three values, one promise

Simplicity

A financial tool shouldn't be intimidating. We designed a clean, welcoming and intuitive interface where everyone feels at home from the very first moment.

Privacy

Your financial information is yours alone. WorthOne performs all calculations inside your browser – the data is never sent to any server and is not stored by us.

Transparency

We are a tool for presenting information and perspective – not advisors. We believe that when you see the full picture, you make the decisions that are right for you.

Important to know

WorthOne is an information and illustration tool only, and does not constitute financial advice, investment advice or a substitute for personalized professional advice. Before making significant financial decisions, we recommend consulting a licensed professional.
